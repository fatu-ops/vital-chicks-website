Vital Chicks & Feeds

Vital Chicks & Feeds operates as a production-led animal feed manufacturer, with manufacturing and quality systems designed to support quality products for domestic distribution and export markets. Our focus is on maintaining control at every stage of the process to ensure predictable quality, safety, and performance.

Raw Material Evaluation & Traceability

Feed quality begins with raw material control. Raw materials entering production are:

  • Evaluated for nutritional relevance and safety
  • Managed to minimise contamination and degradation
  • Tracked through internal handling and production records
Feed Formulation & Performance Alignment

Feed formulations are developed with practical production and usage conditions in mind. Rather than relying on fixed templates, compositions are adjusted to suit:

  • Livestock type and production stage
  • Climate and environmental conditions
  • Intended outcomes such as growth, fattening, or milk yield
  • Distribution and export handling requirements
Quality Assurance & Product Testing

Quality assurance is integrated into the production process. Protocols are applied to verify:

  • Nutritional consistency
  • Moisture and stability parameters
  • Compliance with safety benchmarks
  • Finished product integrity
Certifications & Compliance

Vital Chicks & Feeds structures its manufacturing and quality systems in line with feed safety and quality management principles. Our production approach places emphasis on:

  • quality procedures
  • hygiene and safety controls
  • traceability and record-keeping
  • consistency with regulatory and import-market expectations
Working With Buyers

We work with domestic distributors and export buyers who value clarity, consistency, and manufacturing discipline. Our role is to provide dependable feed production supported by transparent processes and straightforward communication.
