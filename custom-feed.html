Custom Feed Manufacturing for Specific Requirements

Vital Chicks & Feeds develops customised animal feed solutions to meet specific livestock, environmental, and production requirements. Customisation allows buyers to align feed more closely with operational goals, climate conditions, and distribution needs (particularly for export and specialised farming systems).

Area Description Key Points
When Custom Feed Is Required Custom feed solutions are typically developed for:
  • livestock systems with specific nutritional needs
  • farms operating under challenging climate conditions
  • export markets with defined handling or performance requirements
  • buyers seeking formulation adjustments within commercial feed programs
Scope of Customisation Custom feed development may include adjustments to:
  • crude protein and energy balance
  • ingredient selection and ratios
  • fibre, fat, and mineral composition
  • pellet size and physical feed characteristics
  • species-specific or stage-specific requirements
Multi-Species Capability Custom feed solutions can be developed for:
  • poultry (broiler, layer, breeder)
  • cattle (milking, fattening, growing)
  • fish (herbivorous and carnivorous species)
  • goats and camels
  • other livestock categories where tailored formulations are required
Working With Buyers Custom feed development is conducted in collaboration with buyers to ensure clarity on objectives, constraints, and expectations.
  • Focus on practical, scalable solutions
  • Designed for consistency and long-term supply
  • Aligned with buyer requirements and constraints

Working With Buyers

Custom feed development is conducted in collaboration with buyers to ensure clarity on objectives, constraints, and expectations. The focus remains on practical, scalable solutions that can be supplied consistently over time.
