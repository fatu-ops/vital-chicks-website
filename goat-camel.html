Goat & Camel Feed Product Range

Our feed formulations for goats and camels are developed to support fattening and maintenance requirements across different production environments.

Product Description Key Specifications
Goat Feed Designed to support healthy growth and body condition in goat production systems.
  • Balanced crude protein to support muscle development
  • Controlled energy levels for steady weight gain
  • Formulated to support digestion and feed efficiency
Camel Feed Developed to support growth and fattening in camels, particularly in arid and semi-arid regions.
  • Balanced protein and energy to support body condition
  • Formulated with attention to environmental stress factors
  • Designed for practical feeding systems
Goat & Camel Hi-Gro (Fattening) Specialised feed developed for fattening programs in goats and camels.
  • Balanced nutrition to support weight gain
  • Designed for commercial fattening cycles
  • Suitable for use in controlled feeding programs

Nutritional Approach

Goat and camel feed formulations are developed with attention to:

  • Crude protein to support growth and muscle development
  • Energy for weight gain and maintenance
  • Fibre balance for digestive health
  • Minerals and vitamins to support overall condition

Formulations are adjusted based on species requirements, climate conditions, and feeding practices.

Performance & Feed Management

Feed is developed to support:

  • steady growth and fattening performance
  • maintenance of body condition
  • efficient feed utilisation under practical feeding systems

Performance depends on animal management, feeding protocols, and environmental conditions.
