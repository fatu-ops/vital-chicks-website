Vital Chicks & Feeds

Animal feed manufacturing company producing reliable feed solutions for poultry, livestock, aquaculture, and specialty animals—built on hands-on farming roots and decades of production experience.

Company Timeline

A clear view of how the business evolved—from farm-level involvement to structured feed manufacturing and export supply.

1991
Where We Started

The business began in 1991 under Namdar (Pvt.) Limited, with direct involvement in broiler and breeder farming alongside in-house feed production. Working at farm level shaped our understanding early on: feed quality only matters if it delivers results on the ground.

Our early years continue to influence how we approach feed manufacturing today.

Image Placeholder (1991)
2003
Establishment of Vital Chicks & Feeds

In 2003, feed operations were formalised under the name Vital Chicks & Feeds, with a clear focus on manufacturing dependable animal feed for commercial use. As demand grew, so did our product range, expanding beyond poultry to include cattle, fish, and other livestock feeds.

This period laid the groundwork for structured production and long-term supplier relationships.

Image Placeholder (2003)
2015
Strengthening Quality & Production Standards

By 2015, the company entered a phase of operational maturity. Feed compositions were refined to improve feed efficiency, support animal health, and deliver predictable results across different production cycles.

Greater emphasis was placed on quality control, consistency, and adjusting feed to suit local climate and farming conditions.

Image Placeholder (2015)
Today
Supplying Domestic and Export Markets

Today, Vital Chicks & Feeds manufactures animal feed for a wide range of applications, serving farms and distributors within Pakistan while also supplying export buyers in neighbouring and regional markets.

Product range includes:

  • Poultry feed for broilers, layers, and breeders
  • Cattle feed for milking, fattening, and growing animals
  • Fish feed for herbivorous and carnivorous species
  • Goat and camel feed
  • Custom feed solutions tailored to specific requirements

Looking Ahead

As farming practices and market expectations evolve, our focus remains on steady improvement: strengthening formulations, maintaining production discipline, and supporting exporters with feed they can rely on.
