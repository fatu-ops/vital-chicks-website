Nutritional Approach

Cattle feed formulations are developed with attention to:

  • Crude protein to support milk production and growth performance
  • Energy balance to support fattening and lactation needs
  • Calcium and phosphorus for skeletal and metabolic support
  • Total Digestible Nutrients (TDN) for feed efficiency

Formulations are adjusted based on production stage, feeding practice, and environmental conditions.

Performance & Feed Management

Cattle feed is developed to support:

  • improved milk yield and consistency
  • steady weight gain in fattening operations
  • maintenance of animal condition
  • efficient feed utilisation

Performance depends on herd management, feeding practices, and environmental factors, and feed is designed to integrate into well-managed production systems.
