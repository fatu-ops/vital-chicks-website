Fish Feed Product Range

Our fish feed formulations are designed to meet the nutritional requirements of different species and feeding behaviours, with attention to consistency and practical farm application.

Product Description Key Specifications
Feed for Herbivorous Fish Developed for species that rely primarily on plant-based nutrition. Suitable for freshwater aquaculture systems and managed feeding programs.
  • Balanced protein levels to support growth
  • Controlled carbohydrates for energy utilisation
  • Formulated to support digestion and feed efficiency
Feed for Carnivorous Fish Formulated to meet the higher protein and energy requirements of carnivorous species. Suitable for intensive and semi-intensive aquaculture environments.
  • Balanced protein to support muscle development
  • Controlled lipids for energy density
  • Designed to support growth performance and feed conversion

Nutritional Approach

Fish feed formulations are developed to provide a balanced combination of:

  • Protein for growth and tissue development
  • Lipids for energy supply
  • Carbohydrates for metabolic support
  • Phosphorus for skeletal development
  • Vitamins and minerals for overall fish health

Nutrient balance is adjusted based on species type, feeding behaviour, and farming conditions.

Performance & Feed Management

Fish feed is developed to support:

  • steady growth performance
  • efficient feed utilisation
  • reduced feed waste
  • stable water quality when used under appropriate feeding practices

Performance depends on species selection, water management, stocking density, and feeding protocols.
