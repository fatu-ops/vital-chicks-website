Poultry Feed: Product Range

Our poultry feed range covers all major production stages, with formulations adjusted to meet nutritional requirements at each phase.

Product Purpose Key Specifications
Broiler Starter Feed Designed to support early growth and development in broiler chicks.
  • Crude protein: 20–22%
  • Balanced minerals, vitamins, and amino acids to support early-stage performance
Broiler Grower Feed Formulated to support optimal growth during the intermediate production stage.
  • Crude protein: 19–20%
  • Supports steady weight gain and efficient feed utilisation
Broiler Finisher Feed Developed to support final-stage growth and finishing performance.
  • Crude protein: 18–19%
  • Designed to support uniform finishing and product quality
Breeder Feed Formulated for breeder operations, focusing on production stability and bird condition.
  • Supports production performance and hatchability
  • Developed to maintain overall health and reproductive efficiency
Layer Feed Manufactured to support egg production throughout the laying cycle.
  • Crude protein: 16–17%
  • Designed to support eggshell quality, egg output, and optimal egg size

Performance & Feed Management

Poultry feed formulations are developed to support:

  • efficient feed utilisation
  • stable weight gain and production outcomes
  • flock condition and health management
  • consistent results across production cycles

Performance is influenced by farm management, environment, and feeding practices, and feed is designed to integrate into well-managed production systems.
